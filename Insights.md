# 📈 Business Insights

## Overall Sales

- The business generated approximately **₹769.20 Million** in total sales from **3,835** transactions.
- A total of **19,150 mobile phones** were sold during the analysis period.

---

## Brand Performance

- Apple generated the highest overall revenue among all brands.
- Samsung closely followed Apple in total sales.
- OnePlus and Vivo maintained competitive market performance.

---

## Customer Purchasing Trends

- Customers purchased multiple units per transaction, indicating strong demand.
- The average selling price was approximately **₹40,114**, reflecting a mix of premium and mid-range devices.

---

## City Performance

- Delhi recorded the highest revenue among all cities.
- Mumbai ranked second in overall sales performance.
- Major metropolitan cities contributed a significant share of total revenue.

---

## Payment Preferences

- UPI was the most frequently used payment method.
- Debit Card and Credit Card were also widely preferred.
- Cash transactions accounted for the smallest share of total sales.

---

## Product Insights

- iPhone SE emerged as the highest revenue-generating mobile model.
- OnePlus Nord and Galaxy Note 20 also performed exceptionally well.

---

## Customer Satisfaction

- The average customer rating was approximately **3.7 out of 5**, indicating generally positive customer satisfaction.

---

## Recommendations

- Increase inventory for high-demand Apple and Samsung models.
- Focus marketing campaigns on top-performing metropolitan cities.
- Continue promoting digital payment methods, especially UPI.
- Monitor lower-rated products to identify quality or service improvements.
- Introduce loyalty programs to encourage repeat purchases.
